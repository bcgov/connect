This portal details the strategic advantages, corporate compliance frameworks, financial structures, and cross-ministry integrations designed for Ministry Directors, Policy Makers, and Business Leaders looking to modernize public service delivery.

## 1. The Connect Value Proposition

### Proven Stability & Reduced Technical Debt
Leveraging vetted, pre-approved common components on a stable, reliable public cloud infrastructure radically lowers long-term application maintenance costs. By avoiding fragmented, siloed implementations, ministry program areas systematically prevent operational errors and technical debt before they occur.

### Global Interoperability
The platform establishes a seamless, secure fabric that bridges all tiers of government—Federal, Provincial, and Municipal. This interconnected landscape fully operationalizes the **"once only" principle**, ensuring that public data is captured securely a single time and shared appropriately, breaking down traditional multi-ministry friction.

### Scalable & Secure Core Infrastructure
Purpose-built to manage high transaction volumes, the cloud-native computing core provides robust infrastructure tools to build, deploy, adapt, and scale online public services. Security controls are natively baked into the foundation, ensuring complete compliance with strict government corporate IM/IT and privacy standards.

### Comprehensive Omni-Channel Support
Service delivery on the platform extends past digital code. It integrates directly into an established, multi-channel citizen support ecosystem encompassing synchronized web chat, telephone support bureaus, and direct in-person Service BC service windows.

### Enterprise DevOps, Operations & Security
Every integrated application benefits out of the box from institutional operational excellence. This includes automated CI/CD deployment pipelines, continuous application health tracking, Service Level Objectives (SLO) monitoring, transactional tracing, centralized log management, and native machine learning readiness.

## 2. Ecosystem Integrations & The Connected Journey

### Connecting the Journey: An Integrated Ecosystem
The Connect Platform maps citizen and business interactions to broad, real-world **High-Level Key Business Events** rather than isolated database rows. This architectural layout intentionally unifies three major public service sectors:

| 💼 Business Management, Search & Asset Tracking | ⚖️ Compliance, Legal & Court Services | 🌲 Property, Land, Environmental & Heritage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| • BC Business Registration<br>• BC Corporations, Cooperatives & Societies<br>• Personal Property Lien Searches & Registries | • Court Registry Services<br> • Wills Registry<br> • Heritage Conservation Act Permits | • Rural Property Tax Services<br>• Short-Term Rental Registry<br>• Site Registry (Environmental)<br>• Historical Sites |

### The Ecosystem in Action
On the common digital platform, a person establishing a new commercial venture can simultaneously verify if a lien exists on a specialized piece of machinery, register their corporate structure, and secure a federal Business Number from the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). That single business account profile seamlessly grants access to required accounts with other provincial partners and ministries, eliminating the need to re-authenticate or re-type the exact same core information.

## 3. Financial, Payment & Revenue Infrastructure

### Statutory Compliance & Financial Management
The platform provides robust, automated core components for handling official statutory filings, revenue collections, and partner distributions. This structure guarantees a completely transparent, auditable financial data trail that complies fully with provincial regulatory, legal, and financial reporting guidelines.

### Volume & Financial Throughput
The platform handles significant, province-wide transactional scale, actively processing **more than 280k payments a year** across platform registries and digitized services.

### Self-Sustaining Service Fee Model
The continuous evolution of the platform is funded via a legislative service fee structure ($1.50 / $1.00 per transaction) embedded directly into platform filings.

This system guarantees continuous platform improvement and security hardening without requiring frequent capital asset funding injections from individual program areas.

### Automated Daily Revenue Disbursement Architecture
The platform features an automated financial routing mechanism to disburse collected statutory fees daily:

```
[ Public & Staff Transaction ] ➔ [ Payment Services Core ]
                                       │
                ┌──────────────────────┴──────────────────────┐
                ▼                                             ▼
   [ Internal Provincial Partners ]          [ Broader Public Sector / Crown Corps ]
      Disbursed daily via EJV GL Coding        Disbursed via CAS Supplier Records
      e.g., Ministry GL GOVM                       e.g., BC Assessment
```

1. **Internal Provincial Ministries:** Funds are transferred dynamically every day using **Electronic Journal Voucher (EJV)** automated general ledger (GL) coding straight to the partner program’s appropriate ministry accounts.
2. **External Partners & Crown Corporations:** Revenue collected for entities operating outside the immediate provincial core ledger (such as *BC Assessment*) is securely disbursed utilizing **Corporate Accounting System (CAS) supplier records** via electronic funds transfer (EFT).

## 4. Platform Statistics & Insights

### Scale of the Platform Fabric
The platform infrastructure coordinates access and delivery for a massive regional user base:
* **290,000+** Active unique individuals utilizing platform services.
* **211,000+** Secure credential logins driven by the **BC Services Card (BCSC)** mobile identity application.
* **71,000+** Secure business identity logins completed via **BCeID** accounts.
* **7,500+** Internal B.C. Public Service staff and operational accounts managed via **IDIR**.
* **48+** Dedicated API client applications consuming managed gateway services.

### Strategic Priorities
To maintain velocity, the platform branch is driving toward a strict **"Common by Default"** implementation mandate. By eliminating highly customized standalone configurations, onboarding pipelines are streamlined. Current high-priority initiatives include onboarding core *BC Assessment* features and completing integrations for 4 new *Ministry of Environment (ENV)* applications.
